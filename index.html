About me

Hello,
My name is Maxime.
Early life & hobbies
Born in Aubagne near Marseille, I grew up in Paris where I did my studies. Curious by nature, I like to learn and try different things. During my childhood, I played bass guitar in a band and learned how to skate with my friends. I learned a lot about food and still cook a lot. A healthy and sporty lifestyle is my perfect compensation for a challenging workday. Below, you can see one of my delicious creations. 
I'm always interested to share recipes! 
Studies
From an early age, I focused my studies on subjects like economy and marketing. Concentrated on always finding the fastest and most productive way to do things (and also according to my high interest in techniques) I joined my engineering school. I finished my studies by completing a data management program. It was for me, the best way to combine all the fields that I was interested in.
International
I had the chance to study and work in different places such as England and Denmark. I worked in London during the Olympic games in 2012 and studied in Kolding during my ERASMUS term. This was an unforgettable experience that challenged me and taught me a lot about intercultural competence.  Later, I did an internship in marketing and web development in Argentina for two months. Furthermore, I lived and worked in Bratislava, Slovakia for almost 3 years.

 

Do you want to contact me? Feel free to reach me.
