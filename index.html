Work

Data Visualisation

I am currently working with Tableau Server and Azure, combining the two to manage users and data governance. In addition to creating visualizations and dashboards, I ensure proper data governance and security is maintained. I am a certified Tableau Desktop specialist and have hosted an online paid course for EDITIONS ENI, sharing my knowledge and experience with Tableau. I have used Tableau Public for several projects and Tableau Gallery remains one of my top sources of inspiration. As a BI Developer, I am responsible for the front end development of BI solutions. This includes creating data visualizations, interactive dashboards, and reports that provide insights into complex data sets. I have experience in developing efficient data models and ETL processes, as well as optimizing performance and scalability of the BI stack.

My experience with Power BI leasted a year and a half but I’m still part of the PBI communities where I follow the different updates (very frequent). I’ve used the tool to optimize the data visualization @Eurosport. From an Excel-based reporting, we pushed the tool to analyze and understand more our clients, purchase habits, revenue…etc. The aim was to help the top management and the sales team to have better insights and be able to deep dive between YTD and archive.

Finally, I used MicroStrategy @CBRE to analyze occupancy and space data across our portfolio. From scheduled structure reports to dynamic visualization, we covered the different needs of our audience.  

ETL

I had the chance to follow a sketchnoting course that turned out to be very useful for the use of Alteryx. I’m very proud to have access to the tool and will, I hope, still have it in the future.

I used Dataiku when I was at engineering school for a couple of months. Impressed by the data science part, I hope to use it in the future.
